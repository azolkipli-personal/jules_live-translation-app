📖 How to Use

🎤 Microphone Mode

  1. Select "Microphone" as audio source
  2. Choose your translation direction (Japanese ↔ English)
  3. Click "Start Translating"
  4. Allow microphone access when prompted
  5. Speak clearly - your words will be transcribed and translated

🖥️ System Audio Mode (For Online Meetings)

  1. Open your meeting (Teams, Zoom, etc.) in a browser tab
  2. Select "System Audio" as audio source
  3. Click "Start Translating"
  4. When prompted, select the tab with your meeting
  5. Important: Check the "Share audio" checkbox!
  6. Audio from the meeting will be transcribed and translated

💡 Tips

  • For best results, reduce background noise
  • Speak one at a time for clearer transcription
  • Use "Export" to save your transcript
  • Sessions auto-restart if recognition times out
